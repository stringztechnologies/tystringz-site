## Genius

### How to Claim the Artist Page

1. Go to https://genius.com/artists/Tystringz
   - If the page doesn't exist yet, create it by adding a song first
2. To claim the page as an official artist:
   - Go to https://genius.com/verified-artists
   - Click "Apply for Verification"
   - You'll need to verify via official social media (Twitter or Instagram)
   - Link your tystringz.com website
3. Once verified, you can:
   - Add official annotations (explain your own lyrics)
   - Pin songs
   - Add artist bio and photos
