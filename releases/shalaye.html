Shalaye

TyStringz

"Shalaye" is the song that started it all. TyStringz's debut single entered the iTunes Worldwide chart at #2, announcing the arrival of a new voice in Afrobeats with the kind of splash most independent artists only dream of. In Yoruba, "shalaye" means to explain yourself, to clarify — and this track was TyStringz's declaration of intent to the world.

Charting History

Debuting at #2 on iTunes Worldwide is a rare achievement for any artist, let alone an independent one operating without major label backing. "Shalaye" sat alongside tracks from established global artists, proving that TyStringz's grassroots fanbase and organic buzz could translate into commercial chart performance. The single's success opened doors that remain open today.

The Sound

"Shalaye" is pure Afropop — melodic, rhythmic, and emotionally resonant. TyStringz's multi-instrumental background shines through in the production: live guitar lines weave through programmed drums, creating a texture that feels both organic and contemporary. The song balances vulnerability with confidence, asking the listener to understand while simultaneously not needing their approval.

Legacy

As a debut single, "Shalaye" set the tone for everything that followed. It established TyStringz as an artist who could compete on a global stage while maintaining the authenticity of his Lagos roots. The track continues to be a cornerstone of his live performances and remains one of the most-requested songs at shows across Austin and beyond.
