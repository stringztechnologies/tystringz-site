Jara ft. Jeff Akoh

ft. Jeff Akoh

"Jara" brings together TyStringz and the immensely talented Jeff Akoh for an Afrobeats collaboration that showcases the best of both artists. The word "jara" in Nigerian parlance means a bonus or extra — something given freely, beyond what's expected. This track is exactly that: a generous offering of melody, rhythm, and vocal prowess.

The Jeff Akoh Connection

Jeff Akoh is one of Nigeria's most gifted vocalists — a talent show winner whose voice carries the kind of emotional weight that stops you mid-step. Pairing his rich, soulful delivery with TyStringz's production sensibility creates something special: a track that moves your body while stirring your emotions.

Production & Sound

"Jara" sits in the sweet spot between Afropop and R&B-influenced Afrobeats. The production is lush but never cluttered — every element has its place. TyStringz's six-instrument proficiency shows in the arrangement: subtle piano chords, guitar textures, and percussion patterns that reference both contemporary Afrobeats and classic Nigerian highlife. The result is a song that feels timeless rather than trendy.

What Jara Means

In Lagos market culture, "jara" is the extra tomato the seller tosses into your bag, the unexpected bonus that makes you smile. TyStringz applies this concept to love and generosity of spirit — giving more than expected, loving beyond requirement. It's a theme that resonates across cultures, which is why "Jara" connects with listeners whether or not they speak Yoruba.
