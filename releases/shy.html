Shy ft. L.A.X

TyStringz · ft. L.A.X

"Shy" is a standout collaboration between TyStringz and L.A.X, the Starboy Records-affiliated artist known for his smooth Afropop delivery and hit records across West Africa. The track explores the tension between attraction and hesitation — that moment when you want to make a move but something holds you back.

The L.A.X Factor

L.A.X (Damilola Afolabi) has been a significant figure in the Afrobeats landscape, with associations with Wizkid's Starboy Records and a string of hits that have defined Nigerian pop music. His presence on "Shy" brings star power and vocal chemistry, creating a dynamic where both artists push each other to deliver their best performances.

Theme & Lyrics

"Shy" captures a universal emotion: the paralysis of attraction. We've all been there — seeing someone across the room, feeling the pull, but being unable to bridge the gap. TyStringz and L.A.X turn this vulnerability into a groove, proving that some of the best Afrobeats comes from honest emotional expression rather than bravado.

The Music Video

The official music video for "Shy" matches the song's push-pull dynamic with visual storytelling that's both stylish and emotionally resonant. The cinematography plays with light and shadow, proximity and distance — visual metaphors for the lyrical content. It's one of TyStringz's most accomplished visual works.
