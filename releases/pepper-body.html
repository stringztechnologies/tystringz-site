Pepper Body ft. Skales

TyStringz · ft. Skales

"Pepper Body" is TyStringz at his most uninhibited — a multilingual Afro-dance anthem that blends English, Pidgin, and Spanish into a single, infectious groove. Featuring Nigerian Afrobeats heavyweight Skales (53M+ streams on "Shake Body," Adidas global campaign), the track is a celebration of confidence, magnetism, and unapologetic self-expression.

The Making of Pepper Body

The song was born on a random Tuesday night in Austin, Texas. TyStringz wrote the hook while experimenting with an Amapiano-influenced bounce, humming the melody into his phone at 2 AM. Two weeks later, the full production came together in a single overnight session — layered percussion, synth stabs, and a bass line heavy enough to make neighbors bang on the wall.

Skales received the demo and returned his verse in three days. No revisions needed. His delivery on the second verse brought exactly the energy the track demanded — instinctual, groovy, and effortlessly cool.

Multilingual by Nature

The language-switching in "Pepper Body" — English, Pidgin, Spanish — isn't calculated. It reflects TyStringz's reality: growing up in Lagos switching between Yoruba, English, and Pidgin, then adding Spanish to the mix after moving to Austin. The Spanish ad-libs weren't planned — they emerged naturally in the studio and stayed because music doesn't respect borders.

Road-Tested Globally

Before its official release, "Pepper Body" was road-tested in clubs across East Africa — Nairobi, Dar es Salaam, Kampala. DJs played early copies and the response was immediate: dance challenges, viral videos, and organic buzz before a release date was even set.

"Pepper Body" dropped February 13, 2026. In Nigerian slang, "pepper" means you're hot, you're causing reactions. "Pepper Body" is that energy in human form — confidence without arrogance, Lagos-born swagger filtered through Austin cool.
